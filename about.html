About NEN A&A

NEN A&A or NEN Apparel & Accessories originated from the deep heavens of the earth when the first plant sprouted out of the ground. It didn’t got enough attention until May 2024, that’s when the shop skyrocketed. The shop was founded by Nathan Sierte, Erika Shook and Norel Mangaliag; they continued to keep the shop alive by changing the products based on what’s trending.
